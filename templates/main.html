This WebApp provides a way in which you can visualize the current cycling infrastructure In Vienna, Austria. To view different map layers, click on the top left layers button for all groups with available layers to be displayed. To choose a specific layer, you have to press on the group button and then press on the layers shown below.
When a layer is visualised on the map, its legend and plot are accessible on the top right screen, if they exist. Also, when clicking anywhere on the layer's features, certain properties are being displayed in a popup.
